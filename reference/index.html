How we work

Process is just
discipline made
repeatable.

We don't have a framework to sell you. We have a set of habits that we've found produce good work — refined across five years of shipping products for teams that care about craft.

01

Understand

We begin by listening more than talking. What does success look like? Who are we building for? What are the actual constraints — not imagined ones? The first week is the most important week of any project, and we treat it that way.

02

Design

We prototype fast and with low fidelity. The goal isn't a beautiful deck — it's an honest answer to whether this will work. We'd rather find the hard problem in Figma than in production, and we'd rather show you something rough that's right than something polished that's wrong.

03

Build

Careful, test-driven development. We move deliberately and document as we go. We prefer simple solutions that age well over clever ones that don't. Code is written for the next engineer, not just the current deadline.

04

Ship

We don't hand over a zip file and disappear. Launch is the beginning of the learning cycle. We stay close for the first month, watch what the data says, and make sure what we built actually does what we said it would.
